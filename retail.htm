My First Retail Job: Working at Movies@ Gorey

During one of my summer holidays, I decided to construct a CV and hand it in to local businesses in hopes of landing a summer job. A few weeks later, I was offered a position at Movies@ Gorey, my local cinema.

Over the course of about 3.5 years working there, I gained valuable experience in retail, particularly in communication, teamwork, and patience.

One of the busiest times was on Cinema Day. A day when all prices are reduced, attracting large crowds and creating a fast-paced, high-pressure environment.

Working on days like this required me to be:

  • Very calm
  • Collected
  • Prepared for anything
  • An effective communicator and team player

These soft skills are especially important because staying calm and communicating clearly under pressure translate directly to software development environments, particularly in agile teams where coordination and adaptability are essential.
