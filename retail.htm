Welcome on this page of my portfolio you are going to see what I did in my first retail job

During one of my summer holidays I decided to construct a cv to hand in to retail jobs to try and get a small job for summer.

I handed in my cv to a handful of companies and a few weeks later I got a a job request from Movies @ Gorey, this is my local cinema.

During my time working for Movies @ Gorey I picked up very good communication and people skills, and in some cases patience. I worked for about 3 and a half years in Movies @ Gorey, so I have some experience in the retail work.

For most cinema's there is something called a cinema day.
What does this mean?
Cinema day is a day where the cinema reduces all of there prices for everything that they sell. Because of this, many people decide to go to the cinema on that day only, this results in a lot of mess and work to be completed.

I am mentioning all of this information because during these times, is when you have to be:

  • Very calm
  • Collected
  • Prepared for anything
  • Communicate effectively as a team
These skills are also incredibily important for the development of software, so if you are able to be calm, collected etc in a retail environment, you should be able to do so in a professional space, such as building software products.

Particularly if you are using an agile approach for developing your software these core skills are used daily.
