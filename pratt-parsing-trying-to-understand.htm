What I’ve Learned About Pratt Parsing

  • Top-down
  • Operator precedence-based (BIDMAS rules)
  • Recursive
  • Tree-structured (produces an AST)
  • Each token has a “binding power” - which determines how tightly it binds to expressions on its left & right. This is what makes it flexible and clean for operator precedence.
