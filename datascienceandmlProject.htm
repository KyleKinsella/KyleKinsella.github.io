Naïve Bayes

During this project, I explored Naïve Bayes. Naïve Bayes is a supervised machine learning model. Naïve Bayes is a generative learning algorithm, this means the model looks for the inputs of a given class or category (Naïve Bayes). There are three types of Naïve Bayes (See below). Types of Naïve Bayes.

  • Gaussian - works on continuous data.
  • Multinomial - works on discrete data.
  • Bernoulli - works on binary/boolean data.

K Means Clustering

During this project, I explored K Means Clustering. K Means Clustering is an un-supervised machine learning algorithm, it is used for clustering groups of data together. In K Means Clustering there is something called a centroid. A centroid is a random plot in the middle of a cluster, the area where the centroid was will change after each iteration of the K Means Clustering model. K Means Clustering.
