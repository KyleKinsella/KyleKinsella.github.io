My Proof of Stake (POS) Algorithm: How Does it Work?

Retrieving a Validator

This is the first and most important aspect of this algorithm. We need to select a validator to validate a block. Below is my way of how I'm going to make my proof of stake algorithm work, for selecting a validator.

  • Step 1: Manually create a text file and within this file, have a handful of Validator addresses and the amount of Stake for each Validator.
  • Step 3: Use one of my functions that I made earlier in the project, to read the file, that contains the validators and stake amounts.
  • Step 4: Chose a random validator weighted by stake (more stake = higher probability of being chosen)
  • Step 5: Whatever validator is selected, they must validate whatever block they are given or they receive.
